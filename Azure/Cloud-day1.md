###### **Why we need the Cloud**



High availability, Fact tolerance and Disaster recovery



Common Goal : HA, FT, DR



###### **Goal :**

* User continue to access application, even when things go wrong.

Common reasons for loss of availability

* Network outage
* Application failure
* System outage
* Power outage
* Catastrophic disaster(Earthquakes,tsunamis)



###### 

###### **High Availabilty and Fault Tolerance:**



* Protect against data centre, server, network and storage subsystems failures to keep your business running without downtime.
* High available system are reliable in the sense that they continue operating even when critical components fail.
* They are also resilient, meaning that they can simply handle failures without service disruption or data loss, and seamlessly recover for such failures
* Azure provide high availability features
* Service Level Agreement
